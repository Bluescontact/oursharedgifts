Refusals

What this practice does not do, and why.

Equal weight to Offerings. The negative space is structural, not apologetic. These are the patterns the design refuses at the design stage — retrofitting after the fact does not work; extraction logic baked into defaults remains operative no matter what surface language is applied.

The eleven patterns cluster as four anti-extraction axes. Each axis is the shadow form of one vertex of the central tetrahedron.

If you find yourself reading this defensively, that's information. The patterns are named to be seen, not to indict anyone in particular.

Capture · shadow of Connection

Taking from receivers without consent or awareness; converting circulating flow into one-way capture.

PatternWhat it looks likeHow the OS refuses it
Attention extractionTools optimized to hold the eye; engagement metrics; notificationsLocal-first tools; plain files; no analytics on personal work
Data extractionCloud tools that learn from inputs to improve their own valueLocal-first; open formats; no telemetry by default
Identity extractionPlatforms that own handle, audience, social graphOwn domain; references on own site; no platform-owned identity

Aggregation · shadow of Differentiation

Collapsing individual distinctness into abstract numerical metrics; replacing recognition with score.

PatternWhat it looks likeHow the OS refuses it
Audience-as-numberFollowers, engagement, reach as success signalNo audience metrics surface; references are named witness statements
Reputation systemsStar ratings, averages, vouching scoresReferences stand individually; no aggregation, no scoring
Relational extractionRelationships turned into marketing channelsNo conversion funnel; no list-building; no relationship-as-pipeline

Scaling · shadow of Architecture

Treating personal scale as preliminary or insufficient; collapsing depth into reach.

PatternWhat it looks likeHow the OS refuses it
Scaling as virtuePersonal scale treated as preliminary to "real" scalePersonal scale named as the correct unit; gift propagates through utility, not scaling
Productization of giftConverting circulating gift into priced commodityGift and exchange kept structurally distinct; no price on gift work
Coherence extractionOutsourcing sense-making to external systemsLibrary is local; framework is self-applied; AI is partner, not author

Performance · shadow of Boundaries

Substituting visible form for actual structural function; performing the boundary rather than holding it.

PatternWhat it looks likeHow the OS refuses it
Container-holder extractionPerson holding the field becomes the absorber of system dysregulationPersonal Governance Model names this directly; OSG architecture is explicit about it
Safety-as-highest-valueExpanding justification for control framed as protectionResilience over safety; brave/emergent space over safe space

Why the four-axis form

The patterns are not eleven random extractions — they cluster as the four shadow operations of the four vertices. Each vertex predicts its own shadow:

  • Connection (value flows between distinct elements) shadows as Capture — flow becomes one-way taking.
  • Differentiation (each element knows what it is) shadows as Aggregation — distinctness collapses into score.
  • Architecture (what works gets built into infrastructure that outlasts the builders) shadows as Scaling — persistence becomes growth, depth becomes reach.
  • Boundaries (limits stated as information) shadows as Performance — structural form replaced by visible performance of form.

This list is not exhaustive. New patterns get added when the body catches one not yet here.
