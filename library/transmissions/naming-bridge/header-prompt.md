# Header Image Prompt — Naming Bridge

For Imagen 4 / Gemini. Run 4 generations, pick the one that holds the register most cleanly. Add any title overlay in post — do not ask the model for text.

## Prompt

> A geometric figure with three luminous vertices in warm gold suspended in deep black space, the apex vertex brightest and the two lower vertices held to it by faint glowing edges, the suggestion of a single form holding three points in alignment, austere and structural and translational. Deep black field with subtle warm shadow, atmospheric and contemplative, single luminous gold geometric form as focal point, soft radial glow at vertices, painterly negative space, minimalist composition with generous emptiness around the form, no text or lettering, no people, no faces, refined and austere, in the spirit of Hilma af Klint's geometric work and Agnes Martin's quiet lines, photographed in a darkened gallery, museum-quality lighting. Centered composition, equal negative space, formal balance, the apex carrying slightly more weight than the base. Aspect ratio 16:9.

## Notes

- Geometry: **vertex-mapped** (three-vertex triangle). The apex should read as the load-bearing point — the structural register that holds philosophical and operational in alignment.
- Mood: austere, structural, luminous, load-bearing, translational.
- If a generation drifts toward four or more vertices, or toward a flat decorative triangle, re-run — the figure must read as one form holding three luminous points, not a logo.
- Lever for adjustment is the subject-and-mood block only. Never weaken the locked aesthetic block.
