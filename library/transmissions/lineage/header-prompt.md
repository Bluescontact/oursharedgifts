# Header Image Prompt — Lineage

## Geometry: layered substrate · Mood: austere, sedimentary, grounded, reverent, structural

## Fully filled prompt (paste into Imagen 4 / Gemini / ImageFX)

> Horizontal strata of luminous gold lines suspended at varying depths in deep black space, brightest at the surface and fading softly into the dark below, the suggestion of layers of ground beneath a single visible surface, austere and sedimentary and reverent. Deep black field with subtle warm shadow, atmospheric and contemplative, single luminous gold geometric form as focal point, soft radial glow at vertices, painterly negative space, minimalist composition with generous emptiness around the form, no text or lettering, no people, no faces, refined and austere, in the spirit of Hilma af Klint's geometric work and Agnes Martin's quiet lines, photographed in a darkened gallery, museum-quality lighting. Horizontal composition with the strata layered downward, strong stillness above the surface line, the deepest strata dissolving into the field. Aspect ratio 16:9.

## Notes

- Run 4 generations; pick the one where the topmost line reads as the brightest "surface" and the lower lines clearly recede into depth. The descent is the whole argument — if the strata read as flat parallel lines of equal weight, the depth cue failed; regenerate adjusting the subject-and-mood block toward "receding into darkness."
- Never weaken the locked aesthetic block. The lever is the subject-and-mood slot only.
