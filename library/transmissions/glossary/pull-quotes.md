# Pull-quotes — Glossary

## 1
When a framework's words mean what common usage means, the framework is not yet structurally specific. When they mean something different, the difference is load-bearing.

## 2
The glossary is reference, not synthesis. It names what the framework already holds; it does not build it.

## 3
The vocabulary is grouped by structural relation, not alphabetized. Vertex, edge, face, center — the words are sorted by where they sit in the form.

## 4
Each edge names an integration the form must hold. Each face names what fails when its vertex drops.

## 5
Recognition is structural pickup, distinct from validation. One opens a use on new ground; the other closes a transaction and leaves the work where it was made.
