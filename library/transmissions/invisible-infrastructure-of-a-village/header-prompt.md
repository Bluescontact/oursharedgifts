# Header Image Prompt — The Invisible Infrastructure of a Village

## Imagen 4 / Gemini prompt (paste below)

> Horizontal strata of luminous gold lines at varying depths, a single faint line near the top and a deeper, denser band of brighter strata beneath it, the suggestion of a vast load-bearing reality sitting below the surface, austere and subterranean and threshold-quiet. Deep black field with subtle warm shadow, atmospheric and contemplative, single luminous gold geometric form as focal point, soft radial glow at vertices, painterly negative space, minimalist composition with generous emptiness around the form, no text or lettering, no people, no faces, refined and austere, in the spirit of Hilma af Klint's geometric work and Agnes Martin's quiet lines, photographed in a darkened gallery, museum-quality lighting. Horizontal composition with the faint line high and the luminous strata weighted toward the lower field, sense of depth and weight beneath a thin surface. Aspect ratio 16:9.

## Notes

- Geometry: layered substrate. The image-able claim is depth — the visible surface is thin and faint; the load-bearing strata are below and brighter.
- Mood: austere, subterranean, load-bearing, threshold-quiet, luminous.
- Composition lever: keep the visible line near the top and faint; let the substrate carry the light. If a generation centers everything evenly, push the mood block toward "weighted toward the lower field" and re-run.
- Run 4 generations. Pick the one where the surface reads as the smaller portion. Add the title overlay in post — do not request text in the image.
