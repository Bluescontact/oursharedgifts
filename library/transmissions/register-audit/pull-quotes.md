# Pull-Quotes — Register Audit

## 1
Register is structural, not stylistic. A piece in the wrong register reads as the wrong thing — even when the content is correct.

## 2
Each coupling opens the next. Catching the drift at the first slip is dramatically cheaper than catching it at the fifth.

## 3
The register is what the receiver actually metabolizes. The content is what the writer thought they were transmitting.

## 4
A framework's content can be correct and its register wrong. The surfaces will then propagate the register, not the content.

## 5
Gatekeeping converts gift form into extraction. The framework still names itself in commons language while its operational logic extracts from receivers rather than circulating with them.
