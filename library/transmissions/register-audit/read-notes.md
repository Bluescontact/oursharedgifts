# Register Audit — Structural Read

## Load-bearing distinction(s)

- **Register vs. content.** The piece insists these are not the same. Content can be correct while register is wrong; the receiver metabolizes the register, not the content. A piece in the wrong register reads as the wrong thing even when the content is right.
- **Trace vs. debris** (secondary). What the metabolized substrate leaves visible in the form, versus construction-stage information the reader does not need.
- **Catching drift early vs. late.** The cost is not flat. Coupling 1 is cheap to catch; Coupling 5 requires undoing five compounded layers.

The load-bearing structural figure is **the drift cascade**: register drift is never a single move — it is a five-stage chain where each slip opens the next, and cost compounds as it descends.

## Chosen geometry

**Layered substrate, rendered as a descending cascade.** The argument is a directional chain of five couplings, each opening the next: Voice → Metrics → Performance → Productization → Gatekeeping → Extraction. It is not two terms held apart (distinction), not a boundary crossed once (threshold), not a closed return (circuit). It is strata descending — surface drift compounding downward into category shift, with the cost of return increasing at each layer. The layered template's parallel horizontal strata are the base; reworked into a stepped diagonal descent with a luminous coupling-node at each junction. One concept only: THE DRIFT CASCADE.

## Naming phrase

**The Drift Cascade** — register drift propagates through five couplings; catch the first slip before it compounds.

## Pull-quote candidates

1. Register is structural, not stylistic. A piece in the wrong register reads as the wrong thing — even when the content is correct.
2. Each coupling opens the next. Catching the drift at the first slip is dramatically cheaper than catching it at the fifth.
3. The register is what the receiver actually metabolizes; the content is what the writer thought they were transmitting.
4. A framework's content can be correct and its register wrong; the surfaces will then propagate the register, not the content.
5. Gatekeeping converts gift form into extraction — the framework still names itself in commons language while its operational logic extracts.
