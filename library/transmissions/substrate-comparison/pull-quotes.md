## 1

The geometry is the same. The medium changes.

---

## 2

Read the rows, not the columns. The vertex pattern is what repeats; the substrate is only where its medium changes.

---

## 3

The four vertices are not stages or a hierarchy. They are four roles the form must hold at once.

---

## 4

A gift left in the wrong medium is not picked up. It cannot do its work. The medium is part of the gift.

---

## 5

The same move — a withdrawal that deposits a gift at the boundary — runs across the body, the organization, the intimate field, and the built system. Each demands its own medium.
