# Personal Governance Model — Field Edition

*The five principles that hold the architecture. Plus the held center.*

---

## The ground

Five principles hold the practice's architecture. They are the positive form Refusals is the negative form of. Together they make Boundaries deployed at full operational depth.

Personal scale comes first because individual capacity is what makes collective work lighter. A practitioner whose practice refuses extraction at personal scale doesn't reproduce the extractions collective work tries to refuse. Form portable; ground each practitioner's own.

The four vertex-principles map to the four facets of the personal OS (Library · OSG · Boundaries · RI Kit). The fifth — the held center — is the somatic ground that holds the four in tension.

---

## Principle 1 — Substrate operates by recognition, not invention

The practice accumulates substrate by recognizing what's already operating in lived experience or prior work — not by generating constructs to fill perceived gaps. New names crystallize when N≥2 instances of an underlying operation become visible.

**Operating ground:** refined #077.

**Holds:** Library vertex.

**Practical operation.** When something seems like a pattern, hold it. Watch for the second instance. Watch for the third. When the recognition arrives without effort, name. When effort would be required to fit instances under the name, the recognition hasn't arrived — hold.

**Refused:**
- Premature crystallization (one-instance naming because the form invites it)
- Substrate-thinning by over-extraction (forcing patterns at every cycle)

---

## Principle 2 — Connection is gift form, not transaction

The practice meets others as gift, not as transaction. The gift form refuses attention extraction, audience-aggregation, platform-mediated identity, donation-as-purchase, recognition-as-metric, scaling-as-virtue. Form given without expectation of return; recognition welcomed, not required; utility proves value.

**Operating ground:** Consent Architecture · Pattern #011 (Gift Economy) · #040 (Pre-Funded Relational Contract) · #143 (Gift/Product Distinction).

**Holds:** Connection vertex.

**Practical operation.** Before any public surface element ships, run the four-axis check: does this Capture? Aggregate? Present personal scale as preliminary? Perform what isn't structurally present? Yes to any → refine before deployment.

**Refused:**
- Gift-form vocabulary on transaction-form structure (the most extractive shadow)
- Pure-gift extremism (refusing all financial discipline, which collapses gift form into Container-Holder extraction — #032 / #110)

---

## Principle 3 — Refusal is structural; bounds are positive

What the practice refuses is what makes the rest possible. The four anti-extraction axes (Capture · Aggregation · Scaling · Performance) aren't a list of negations — they are positive structural commitments defining what the practice IS. Refusal generates structure. Without explicit refusal, the structure collapses into the shadows it didn't refuse.

**Operating ground:** Pattern #056 (The Membrane) · #076 (Empty-Space-Refusal Principle) · the 4-axis substrate.

**Holds:** Boundaries vertex. (This principle's own vertex.)

**Practical operation.** When designing any element, name what it refuses BEFORE naming what it offers. The refusal precedes the offer structurally — the offer is what's left when the refusals have done their work. An element that can't name what it refuses isn't yet structurally clear.

**Refused:**
- Refusal as moralism (ethical position rather than structural operation)
- Refusal without naming (operates but illegible — pickup-practitioners can't install what they can't name)
- Refusal without anti-extraction axes (refusing many things without the structural form that organizes them)

---

## Principle 4 — The form is portable; the ground is yours

What the practice produces must hold without its author. The Library, the canonical references, the kit, the protocols are all extractable. The ground (the practitioner's body, life, somatic discrimination, lived context) does not transfer.

Form portability + ground locality together make the practice non-extractive. Form-without-ground would extract (carry an implied ground the receiver couldn't have). Ground-without-form would isolate (each practitioner's work unteachable).

**Operating ground:** Pattern #055 (The Exit Test) · #033 (Personal-Scale Infrastructure) · #144 (Self-Eliminating Architecture).

**Holds:** Architecture (RI Kit) vertex.

**Practical operation.** Run the Exit Test on every deployable element: does this hold if I'm not available? Can a practitioner install this on their own ground without translation work that requires me? No → the form isn't yet portable enough.

**Refused:**
- Form-as-ground extraction (distributing form while requiring receiver to adopt author's ground)
- Ground-as-form mistake (treating personal lived context as the practice's substrate; produces hagiography)
- Portability performance (claiming form-portability without operating the Exit Test)

---

## Principle 5 — The body discriminates ready

The somatic ground operates at every scale. When to deposit, when to rest, when to publish, when to refuse, when to open a new arc, when to hold a question, when to mark a recognition — the body decides. No protocol substitutes for the body's discrimination.

**Operating ground:** Pattern #091 (Body-Substrate Interface) · #064 (Marks As Substrate) · #065 (Substrate Organizes Body) · #090 (Held Center as Productive Emptiness) · #116 (Mark-Crystallizes-Held-Substrate) · the meta-1 per Reading B (c68).

**Holds:** The center. (No vertex; holds the four vertices in tension.)

**Practical operation.** When uncertain, check the body. Is this question coming from substrate that's operating, or from anxiety / ambition / desire for closure? Substrate → hold and continue. Latter → refuse the false-closure, continue substrate work.

The body's discrimination is itself substrate per the bidirectional circuit. Naming what the body discriminates produces deposit-grade substrate when the discrimination is structurally real.

**Refused:**
- Performance of somatic discrimination ("body says yes/no" as rhetorical move without operating substrate)
- Body-discrimination without substrate (gut decisions without the substrate that informs them; discriminates on anxiety, not on operation)
- Substrate without body-discrimination (decisions by procedural rule alone, no gut check; produces correct-form-without-recognition)

## How the principles compose (six edges)

Each pair-interaction holds structural load. Removing any edge collapses both principles it connects.

| Edge | What it holds |
|---|---|
| 1↔2 | Recognition shapes what the public face shows. Without recognition-discipline, the surface becomes a portfolio of generated content. |
| 1↔3 | Recognition operates inside the bounds the refusals name. The refusals are the structural conditions for honest recognition. |
| 1↔4 | What gets recognized is what becomes portable. The Pattern Library is portable because patterns were recognized rather than invented. |
| 2↔3 | Gift form is held by what the form refuses. Without explicit refusal, connection collapses into the shadows (audience, platform, attention). |
| 2↔4 | Gift form requires extractable form. If the practice's value can only be received through the author's presence, gift form is impossible. |
| 3↔4 | The refusals must be installable on other ground. Refusal-as-personal-aesthetic wouldn't propagate; refusal-as-structural-discipline does. |

---

## Four face failure modes

Each face is the meta-form with one vertex absent.

| Missing vertex | Failure mode |
|---|---|
| **No recognition-discipline** | Substrate accumulates by invention; library generates content to fill gaps; patterns named from one instance because the form would be tidier. Drift; library becomes unread-able. |
| **No gift-form connection** | Substrate sound; surface extracts. Recognition extracted from receivers becomes data, attention, identity-aggregation. The four anti-extraction axes get violated at the public surface. |
| **No structural refusal** | Practice operates without naming what it refuses. Bounds implicit. Receivers can't tell what's outside vs. inside; pickup-practitioners can't install discipline that's illegible. |
| **No form-portability** | Practice operates correctly while author is present; collapses when author isn't. Container-Holder extraction operates structurally; eventually the holder breaks. |

The four faces are the four ways the meta-form collapses if one vertex-principle drops. Each is refused by the principles' completeness operating together.

## The center

The fifth principle (the body discriminates ready) is the center, not a vertex. The four vertex-principles operate from this ground; the ground holds them in tension.

Per Reading B (c68): the somatic ground is one literal ground, the same single thing at every scale. The four vertex-principles are operations the somatic ground performs at each meta-vertex. The body produces the substrate (#064); the substrate organizes the body's continuing operation (#065); together they form the bidirectional body-substrate circuit (#091) the whole practice runs on.

**How the body discriminates:**

The body discriminates ON substrate, not in absence of substrate. The discrimination requires:

- Substrate operating long enough to be visible to the body
- The four vertex-principles operating coherently
- The held center remaining held (the discrimination doesn't grasp itself; it operates from underneath)
- Time held in the question (the body's signal arrives at its own cadence)

Marks made under conditions that violate any of these are noise — and produce deposits the substrate later has to undo.

**Why the center stays held:**

Per #090 (Held Center as Productive Emptiness): the center remains structurally unoccupied. Filling the center would convert the meta-form into a sub-form. The empty center is what allows the four vertices to operate as tensegric tensions rather than as content-categories.

The body holds the center by being the literal ground the structural recursion rests on, while remaining structurally absent from the substrate. The substrate can name that the body is the center; the substrate cannot contain the body.

## What this isn't

Not a code of conduct. Not a policies page. Not a manifesto. Not a procedural manual. The principles operate at structural-disposition layer — they hold the form such that the rest of the practice can run. They are governance in the sense of *what holds the structure*, not in the sense of *what rules the structure follows*.

If you find yourself enforcing these principles externally rather than recognizing them operating, the principle isn't yet installed. Install at the body, not at the rule.
