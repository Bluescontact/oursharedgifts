# Connection × Biological

## Signature

You can be in a room of people you love for hours and feel no different physiologically than you did alone. Hugs land but don't change your state. The gestures of care are present; co-regulation isn't happening. Your body remains in its baseline guardedness regardless of who is near.

## Reading

Movement 2 + **`somatic_practices_lens.md`** (co-regulation, ventral vagal engagement). A body in genuine contact runs a different program than a body in proximity. Until the program runs, the contact has not occurred.

## Practice

**presencing.html** — practice arriving in your body before contact. Do this alone first; the sequence has to be available before another nervous system can be added.

## Intervention

For one week, do not enter a conversation while still finishing the previous one. Pause at the threshold of every contact long enough for your body to actually arrive. The withdrawal is from the rolling-conversation pattern. The gift is the body that has been allowed to land before being asked to engage.

## Expected resistance

*"I don't have time."* — The time was being spent anyway, on contacts that did not deliver. You are reallocating it from many hollow ones to fewer real ones.

## Signal of success

A specific person's nervous system can change yours without you having to think about it. You leave a conversation in a different physiological state than you entered it.
