# Closing

This diagnostic ended where it should end. You have a name for what is missing, a place to read, a practice to try, and a structural move to consider. You do not owe a report. If something develops, that is a return gift, and you decide whether and when to offer it. The framework does not need your data to know it works.

## What this audit does not do

This audit does not diagnose mental health, medical conditions, abusive relationships, organizational misconduct, or anything that requires a different kind of care. If a vertex you scored weak corresponds to a domain where another instrument is the right one, this audit is not in the way of you finding it.
