# Diagnosis — Connection

Your weakest vertex is **Connection** — the capacity for genuine contact across difference.

The structure is sound. Roles are clear. Limits are held. Things get built. And yet something is missing that no audit of process can locate: actual exchange. The group is structured but isolated. People are present but not arriving.

The pattern most likely operating is **The Poison Gift** — the form of exchange is intact, but no one is actually arriving inside it. Gifts get offered, gratitude gets performed, the architecture looks sound. The people inside sense something missing but cannot name it, because every form was observed.

The work is not more meetings. It is fewer meetings where genuine contact is permitted to occur.
