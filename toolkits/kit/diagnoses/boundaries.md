# Diagnosis — Boundaries

Your weakest vertex is **Boundaries** — the capacity for legible limits that serve development.

Energy is moving. People are connecting. There is identity and there is intention. But nothing stays put, because nothing is held. Generosity that doesn't know where to stop becomes depletion. Contact that doesn't know where the membrane is becomes diffusion.

The pattern most likely operating is **The Burnout Cycle.** Someone — possibly you — is performing beyond capacity to maintain a boundary the system refuses to recognize. The structure has already degraded; one person's body is hiding the degradation.

The work is to name a single limit you have been hiding, and let the system meet the reality of it. The crisis that follows is not failure. It is the pressure that makes new architecture possible.
