# Diagnosis — Architecture

Your weakest vertex is **Architecture** — the capacity to build infrastructure that outlasts you.

The relationships are real. The boundaries are clear. The identity is distinct. And yet, year over year, almost nothing accumulates. The same problems recur. The same heroism is required. Beautiful work happens that leaves nothing behind.

The pattern most likely operating is **The Dependency Loop.** One person's capacity has become another party's infrastructure. What looks like generosity is functioning as a ceiling on their development — and on yours.

The work is to identify a single thing you are currently providing that the system has adapted to instead of developing — and to design what you will leave at the threshold when you withdraw.
