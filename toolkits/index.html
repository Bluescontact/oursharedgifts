Toolkits

What persists without Kevin.

The Architecture vertex. Tools that operate the framework in the receiver's hands. No login, no account, no data captured. Each tool reads the receiver's own answers back through the framework — no AI grading, no scoring, no leaderboard.

A tool you use is substrate. A tool you read about is content. Notice which mode you're in right now.

Live

  • Quick Diagnostic Four questions, one per vertex. Twelve minutes. For any system you're inside — a team, a relationship, a project. Reads your own answers back to identify which capacity is weak. Standalone HTML; runs in your browser; no data leaves the page.
  • Fit Filter Consultation intake. Five questions. Routes a prospective receiver to one of three outcomes — fit (proceed to calendar), substrate first (read or run a tool, come back), or outside the consultation tier (refusal as information). Built from consult.html substrate.
  • 48 Recognition Cards Somatic activation across four movements (Differentiation · Connection · Boundaries · Architecture), twelve cards per movement. Each card has four layers: a recognition that mirrors you, an activation that lands it in your body, a seed that compresses the principle, and a transmission that turns you toward someone else. Standalone; runs in your browser; no data leaves the page.
  • RI Kit The full diagnostic instrument. 12-question audit → vertex diagnosis → substrate selection → prescription. Twelve statements rated on a four-point scale; the highest-sum vertex is your weakest; the kit then routes you to the substrate (Biological · Institutional · Relational · Technical) most relevant to your situation; you receive one of sixteen prescriptions specific to your vertex × substrate intersection. Standalone; runs in your browser; no data leaves the page. Editorial source at /toolkits/kit/.

    • Transmission Engine Kevin-side. Converts a finished article into a Substack-ready artifact set — recognition pattern (SVG), header-image prompt for Imagen 4, pull-quote share-cards. Pipeline: structural read → symbolic distillation → header prompt → pull-quotes. First validated run produced the Recognition Threshold artifact set.
