Village Market

Asks and offers within 45 miles of wherever the bus is parked.

Demonstration. The pins below are example asks and offers, here so you can see and feel how the Gift Circle works. Browsing, posting, and claiming are all simulated for now — nothing is sent or stored. When the circle goes live, these become real exchanges within the radius.

What people in the local fabric are asking for and what they have to give. Each submission carries both — one ask and one offer — so nobody shows up to only take or only give. Records live on the map for 72 hours unless the submitter re-ups; after that they expire. First names appear when posters choose to share them.

Current radius: 45 miles around Grass Valley, CA. When the bus moves, the radius moves. See Current for the live location. See the protocol for what this is and isn't.

About this map

Each pin sits at zip-code center, not at a specific address. Asks and offers from the same submitter are offset slightly so both are visible. Contact information is held privately and revealed only to the person who claims a record. Open records appear here; claimed, withdrawn, met, and expired records drop off the public view.

If you want to participate, post an ask and an offer — the submission carries both. If you can meet one of the open asks or take up one of the open offers, click the pin and claim it.

This is Kevin's instance, held from wherever the bus is parked. Anyone can hold their own radius from somewhere else — see the protocol for how.
