How This Works

I build things that are useful to me and document what I learn. The frameworks describe patterns I've observed in genuine coordination—whether between people, institutions, or AI systems. The projects are physical infrastructure that demonstrates these patterns in material form.

Everything here is offered freely because knowledge shouldn't be gated by someone's circumstances. If you find value in this work and want to support continued building, there's a support page. But access is never conditional on payment.

The frameworks propagate through utility, not marketing. If they're useful, they'll spread through recognition. If not, that's information too.
