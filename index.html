Our Shared Gifts

I'm Kevin.

I build things, fix things, and live off-grid near Grass Valley, California.

The bus — a 1995 school bus — is my house and my generator. The solar, about 2.6 kilowatts of it, runs the place and charges the motorcycle. The setup I built provides all of my personal infrastructure — and it was built with excess capacity on purpose, so the bus can offer itself as a mobile infrastructure node: off-grid community power, water, and ice. I weld, I build, I fix, and I figure out the problems no single trade owns.

I keep what I need to live small and owned, so I can do good work without money being the boss of me. That's the whole thing. Three reasons you might be here:
