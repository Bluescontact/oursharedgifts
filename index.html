Our Shared Gifts

The personal practice made legible.

Infrastructure built by Kevin to support Kevin — and to let him share gifts coherently without counting the cost. The site is one working instance of the framework it carries: geometry, gift form, refusal, consent, and recognition, each operating in a surface you can navigate from here. The form above is the whole of it — four vertices, six axes. Everything below is that form, deployed.

Four surfaces, one per vertex

Each surface carries one vertex of the form, and a specific structural role. Each is reachable from anywhere.

  • Library — Differentiation. What the substrate is: the manuscript, the operational lens, the framework texts, case portraits, lineage, transmissions. Read freely; everything is self-licensed.
  • Toolkits — Architecture. What runs in your own hands: a twelve-question audit, a four-question diagnostic, a five-question fit filter, forty-eight recognition cards. No login, no data captured.
  • Offerings & the Gift Circle — Connection. What is given freely — and the local fabric where others give too: asks paired with offers within the radius, so no one shows up to only take or only give.
  • Consultations — Boundaries. Where the framework gets applied to one specific situation, gated and consented. Wednesdays and Thursdays.

Before you click anything, notice what you came here looking for. The site is shaped to surface what fits, not to retain you.

Where to start

No single entry path is required. A few that work, depending on what you came for:

  • For the framework first — The Meta-Tetrahedron walks the operational lens in eight parts.
  • For a specific situation — the Quick Diagnostic reads four questions in your own hands.
  • For the deep diagnostic — the RI Kit walks twelve questions through diagnosis and prescription.
  • For the local fabric — the Gift Circle pairs asks with offers within the radius.
  • For what the practice will not do — Refusals names the eleven extraction patterns the form refuses.
  • For something specific from Kevin — the gift menu names what is offered.

What this is not

Not a business pretending to be a gift. Not a platform. Not a community waiting to be joined. Eleven extraction patterns are named structurally on the Refusals page; the design of every surface descends from refusing them.

Receiving the work

Use freely. Adapt as needed. Credit sources. Recognition welcomed, not required. Utility proves value. The form is portable; the ground is yours — pick it up and hold it on your own.
