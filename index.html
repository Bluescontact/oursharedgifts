I'm Kevin.

I build things, fix things, and live off-grid on a farm near Wheatland, California.

The bus is my house and my generator. The solar runs the place and charges the motorcycle. The pump and tank I built give the farm running water on demand. I make tables, benches, cutting boards, stools. I weld, I repair, I figure out the problems no single trade owns.

I keep what I need to live small and owned, so I can do good work without money being the boss of me. That's the whole thing. Three reasons you might be here:
