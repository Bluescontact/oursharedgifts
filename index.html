Our Shared Gifts

The personal practice made legible.

This is infrastructure built by Kevin to support Kevin, and enable Kevin to share gifts coherently without counting the cost. The site is one working instance of the framework it carries — geometry, gift form, refusal, consent, and recognition all operating in the surfaces you can navigate from here.

Four surfaces

The site holds four functional surfaces, one per vertex of the form the framework rests on. Each surface is reachable from anywhere; each carries a specific structural role.

  • Library — what the substrate is. Manuscript, operational lens, framework texts, case portraits, lineage, transmissions. Read freely; everything is self-licensed.
  • Toolkits — what others can use. Four standalone tools that run the framework in your own hands: a 12-question audit, a quick four-question diagnostic, a five-question fit filter, and 48 somatic recognition cards. No login, no data captured.
  • Offerings — what is given freely in the gift zone. Six offerings, each with what is and what is not offered named in equal weight.
  • Consultations — where the framework gets applied to a specific situation. Wednesdays and Thursdays. Commons-work consent.

Before you click anything, notice what you came here looking for. The site is shaped to surface what fits, not to retain you.

Where to start

The site does not require a single entry path. A few that work depending on what you came for:

  • For the framework first — The Meta-Tetrahedron walks the operational lens in eight parts.
  • For a specific situation — the Quick Diagnostic reads four questions in your own hands.
  • For the deep diagnostic — the RI Kit walks twelve questions through diagnosis and prescription.
  • For what the practice will not do — Refusals names the eleven extraction patterns the form refuses.
  • For something specific from Kevin — the gift menu names what is offered.

What this is not

Not a business pretending to be a gift. Not a platform. Not a community waiting to be joined. Eleven extraction patterns named structurally on the Refusals page; the design of every surface descends from refusing those.

Receiving the work

Use freely. Adapt as needed. Credit sources. Recognition welcomed, not required. Utility proves value. The form is portable; the ground is yours.
