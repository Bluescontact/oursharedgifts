Add to the Gift Circle

No account, no form, no funnel. It comes straight to a person.

Posting here is mediated, on purpose. You email what you want on the map; it routes to Kevin and the back-office, gets placed, and goes live — usually within the hour. Nothing is harvested, and your contact details stay private, shared only with the person on the other end of a connection.

One ask and one offer

Each post carries both — something you could use a hand with, and something you have to give — so nobody shows up to only take or only give. If you only have one right now, send it anyway; the pairing is the spirit, not a gate.
