The Machine

The autonomous back-office. Raw material enters, gets processed, and surfaces at the gate. Kevin appears once — at canon entry. Everything else runs without him.

Why This Exists

A single-person system has one failure mode: the person becomes the bottleneck for everything. The machine exists to prevent that. It handles intake, extraction, routing, and triage — so Kevin's finite attention lands only where it's irreplaceable.

The irreplaceable place: the canon gate. Kevin's felt-read is the only instrument that can hold the calibration zero. Everything upstream of that gate is automatable. Everything downstream of it is already in the world.

The Process

Step 1 Intake Automatic
Raw material lands in _INTAKE/ — voice notes, papers, field observations, fragments. The drop zone accepts everything without filtering. Volume is expected; the machine handles it.
Step 2 Substrate AI — autonomous
The Substrate Skill runs an extraction pass over each item. It looks for transferable structural patterns — the form beneath the instance. Kevin's lived vocabulary, not the contributor's surface language. Output: pattern candidates + fold verdicts + a substrate brief per item.

Kevin doesn't read the raw material. He reads the brief.
Step 3 Converger AI — autonomous
Each candidate is screened against the standing 35-pattern canon. Three verdicts:

COMPOST — folds into an existing pattern, strengthening it.
HOLD — real but needs one specific move before it can promote.
PROMOTE-READY — surfaces to the gate.

The Converger fails toward subtraction. When uncertain, it compostes. The falsifier for stopping: the pile grew.
Step 4 Dispatcher AI — autonomous
The Dispatcher aggregates PROMOTE-READY candidates into a daily brief. One item per brief — the one that's most ready, with the falsifier and a body question already formed. The Governor triage runs here: if the clock or topology isn't right, the item holds.

Kevin opens one file. He reads one thing. He marks.
Step 5 The Gate Kevin — only here
PROMOTE — enters the canon. The pattern is now part of the standing library, released to the field.
HOLD — returned to the queue with the specific blocking condition named.
COMPOST — closes cleanly. The material is metabolized; no residue.

This is the only place Kevin has to stand. The gate is his calibration zero — his body is the instrument. It cannot be delegated, automated, or approximated.
Step 6 Canon The field
The promoted pattern joins the library. It's immediately open source — free to use, develop, and test in any territory. Attribution tracks which fields confirmed the pattern across its lifetime, making the canon stronger with each territory that holds it.

The Governing Principle

A system that converts cost into gift, and improves with cost, can come to require cost. The machine exists to prevent the stack from eating its own substrate — which is the person running it. The Governor sits at intake. The Converger sits at the canon boundary. The Dispatcher sits at Kevin's attention. Three brakes, one direction: protect the body that the whole thing runs on.
