About

Read as infrastructure spec, not biography.

Our Shared Gifts is infrastructure built by Kevin Mears to support Kevin, and enable Kevin to share gifts coherently without counting the cost.

That sentence is the load-bearing claim. Everything on this site descends from it. The architecture is not for an audience and not about a person; it is a structural surface that lets one practitioner give what they have to give without the giving consuming the giver.

Personal scale is the correct unit for this work, not a downgrade from scalable. The Meta-Tetrahedron walks the structural reason in full.

What backs the framework

The Recognition Infrastructure framework was not derived from theory and then field-tested. It was extracted, over twenty years, from practice across several substrates. Each anchor below is substrate the framework rests on, not credential it relies on.

  • Twenty years of partner dance Fusion Dancing, primarily in the social register. Where the framework's Connection vertex got its felt sense — co-regulation as something the nervous system does before the cognitive system narrates it.
  • The bus build A mobile dwelling and shop, built by hand. Substrate behind the framework's Architecture vertex at personal scale — what persists when the builder is no longer holding it together.
  • The solar biochar toilet Distributed-node sanitation infrastructure. One of the case portraits in the manuscript; an instance of the framework operating in the technical substrate at communal scale.
  • Federal whistleblower work Structural integrity work inside an institution. Where the framework's Boundaries vertex earned its weight — limits stated as information, with the institution's full apparatus arrayed against the naming.
  • The DSS cohort Two years of structured human–AI collaboration that crystallized the substrate into the manuscript form. The framework's current readability is downstream of this exchange.

The work is grounded in Grass Valley, California. Personal scale is geographically specific too.

What propagates

The framework does not propagate by carrying its scaffolding to a new practitioner. It propagates by the new practitioner having their own ground that their own tetrahedral forms can rest on.

Same form. Different ground. The framework propagates by recurring around new ground.

This means the operator's biography is not load-bearing for receivers. What's load-bearing is the substrate — and the substrate is on this site, self-licensed, free to read, adapt, and apply.
