About this page

Current State is the only page on the site that depends on real-time fact. The rest of the site is substrate — true regardless of cycle. This page is the one place the practice tells its operating truth.

The aggregation discipline is structural: receivers see operating state; the page does not surface who gave, who received, who is in an active arc. That asymmetry protects the gift form from quietly converting to a relational ledger. See the Refusals page for the eleven extraction patterns this discipline refuses.

If you sent a request and the page indicates closed capacity, the request is held until capacity returns. If you are waiting and the page hasn't updated in months, the body is the discriminator, not the calendar.
