The tool outlives the hand

The edge between Connection and Architecture.

Every artifact is built so the carrier is removable. The kit routes without Kevin. The transmission engine produces without Kevin. The manuscript circulates under a self-license clause that makes Kevin's permission unnecessary. The tool teaches the pattern; the pattern survives the carrier.

This edge names the propagation discipline. A connection that depends on the carrier's continued presence has not yet finished. A connection mediated by a tool that the carrier built — and that runs without the carrier in the loop — is what the architecture is for.

Held position. Substrate composting · receiver-facing surface arriving when ripe. The toolkits surface holds the operational substrate; offerings holds the receiver-facing substrate; the dedicated edge-hub page consolidates these once the dual-build pattern is named explicitly at this scale.
