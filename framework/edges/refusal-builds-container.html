Refusal builds the container

The edge between Differentiation and Boundaries.

What is not yours to give defines the silhouette of what is. Every refusal sharpens differentiation. The eleven extractions named at design stage in the Personal OS map are how the library knows where its outer edge sits.

Refusal does not subtract from the work. Refusal IS the work, at this edge. The silhouette of the gift becomes legible from outside only when the surface around it has been carved by everything the carrier has declined.

Held position. Substrate composting · receiver-facing surface arriving when ripe. The substrate that grounds this edge is rich — eleven refused extractions, thirty-seven refusals across four layers, the register audit, the negative-exemplar registry — but its consolidation into a single edge-hub page is the work of a later cycle.
