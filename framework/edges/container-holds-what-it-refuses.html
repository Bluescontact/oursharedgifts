The container holds what it refuses

The edge between Boundaries and Architecture.

Architecture is durable not because it is hardened but because the refusals are explicit. Every disabled option, every closed door, every named extraction is a structural member of the container. Architecture without enumerated refusal collapses inward into platform shape — the container becomes the trap.

This edge names the load-bearing relationship between what gets refused and what stays standing. The refusals page is not a list of grievances; it is the structural exposure of the architecture's outer walls.

Held position. Substrate composting · receiver-facing surface arriving when ripe. The substrate that grounds this edge is among the richest — the four-layer refusals architecture, the eleven refused extractions, the operating principles, the system-level discipline catalog — and the dedicated edge-hub page is the natural first α-cycle target.
