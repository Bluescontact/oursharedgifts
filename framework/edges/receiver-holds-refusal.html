The receiver who can hold the refusal

The edge between Connection and Boundaries.

A connection that survives a refusal is the only kind of connection that compounds. The Village Market refuses being a platform; the host who can hold that refusal is the host the architecture wants. The receiver who can tolerate the refusal-surface has structural grounds for trust before any exchange occurs.

This edge names the filtering function of refusal at the connection layer. Visitors who cannot hold the refusals self-route away. Visitors who can hold them are already inside the trust the architecture protects.

Held position. Substrate composting · receiver-facing surface arriving when ripe. The Village Market substrate, the refusals page, and the operating principles together hold this edge; the consolidated edge-hub page awaits the substrate's settling.
