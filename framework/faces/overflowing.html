Face · No-Boundaries

Overflowing

The face opposite Boundaries.

When the carrier cannot refuse the demands that arrive, the gift becomes obligation. The Burnout Cycle. The Poison Gift. The carrier depletes; the receivers do not notice; the architecture eats the architect. Cycles getting expensive instead of cheap.

The most seductive failure mode — because it looks like virtue from outside. Generosity without limit reads as the gift form's ideal; it is in fact its collapse. The gift becomes obligation; the obligation becomes depletion; the depletion ends the practice.

Diagnostic signal

The face is occupied when:

  • The carrier cannot name what they refuse without apology
  • Each cycle of work feels more expensive than the last
  • Receivers expect what was once offered freely; the gift register collapses into transaction expectation
  • The carrier's body signals exhaustion that the carrier overrides as commitment

The move that prevents it

Enumerate refusals as legible information. Make the refusals visible to receivers before any exchange. The Capacity Infrastructure Lens specifically surfaces whether cycles are compounding or expanding — overflow registers there cleanly.
