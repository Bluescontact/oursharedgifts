Face · No-Differentiation

Dissolved

The face opposite Differentiation.

When the carrier cannot distinguish what is theirs from what is borrowed, the work becomes generic. The library flattens into reference. The voice drifts into market dialect. The pattern dissolves into the field. Vocabulary recognizable; discipline absent.

This is the failure mode hardest to detect from inside, because dissolution often feels like agreement. When everyone is saying similar things in similar register, the field reads as consensus rather than as collapse of differentiation.

Diagnostic signal

The face is occupied when:

  • Framework vocabulary deploys without the disciplines the vocabulary names
  • The carrier cannot say what their work IS in a sentence that excludes adjacent work
  • What once felt distinct now reads as one of many similar things
  • External readers cannot tell the work apart from its imitators

The move that prevents it

Run the Quick Diagnostic or the Capacity Infrastructure Lens against the work. Both surface vocabulary-without-discipline drift. Then name what is yours specifically — the silhouette becomes visible only when refused and adjacent moves are both legible.
