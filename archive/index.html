The thinking behind it.

The front of this site is the life — what I do, how I live, the model anyone can copy. This is the substrate underneath it: the framework I built the practice on, the writing, the tools. It's here for anyone who wants to dig. It isn't the front door, and you don't need a word of it to ask me for something or to run the model on your own ground.

Where it lives

  • The Library — the framework texts, the manuscript (The Architecture of Coherence), case studies, lineage, the transmission sets.
  • The Framework — the geometry: the walkable tetrahedron, the edges and faces.
  • The Meta-Tetrahedron — the operational lens, in eight parts.
  • Toolkits — the diagnostics that run in your own hands. (Also linked from the footer — those still run.)

Read it if you want the why. Skip it if you just came for the what — that's out front.
